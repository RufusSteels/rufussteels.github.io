This was one of the earlier games I ever made. It's a simple platformer for now. I will however add some more features in the future like combat and enemies.

In this game the focus was primarily on making the movement and the camera feel nice.

This was also a nice mental exercise because I did the math for the collisions and everything from scratch.

Then of course there is the ability to import images to load them as levels as I mentioned before.

Different colours in the images are converted into different tiles.
Other colours are converted to the player character or NPCs.
